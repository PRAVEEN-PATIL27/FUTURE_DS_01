# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics

## 🚀 Project Overview

Business Sales Performance Analytics is a data-driven project developed as part of the **Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship Program**. The project focuses on analyzing sales performance across products, regions, customer satisfaction metrics, and target achievement rates using SQL and Power BI.

The primary objective of this analysis is to transform raw business sales data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making and improve business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall business revenue performance.
* Identify top-performing products and regions.
* Evaluate monthly sales trends.
* Measure target achievement rates.
* Assess customer satisfaction levels.
* Understand the impact of discounts on revenue generation.
* Create an interactive Power BI dashboard for business reporting.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* SQL

* Data Visualization
* Business Analytics

## 🔍 Key Findings

### Revenue Analysis

* Total revenue generated exceeded ₹7.32 million.
* The business demonstrated strong sales performance across multiple regions.

### Product Performance

* Jeans emerged as the highest revenue-generating product.
* Shirts and Jackets also contributed significantly to total sales.

### Regional Performance

* South region generated the highest revenue.
* West region showed comparatively lower sales performance.

### Target Achievement

* Approximately 78.6% of sales targets were successfully achieved.
* This indicates effective sales strategies and operational efficiency.

### Customer Satisfaction

* Customer ratings remained positive with an average rating of 3.81.
* Positive feedback suggests good product quality and customer experience.

### Discount Analysis

* Strategic discounting supported sales growth while maintaining profitability.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase focus on high-performing products such as Jeans.
* Expand successful sales strategies from the South region to other markets.
* Improve performance in lower-performing regions.
* Continue monitoring customer feedback to enhance satisfaction.
* Implement targeted promotional campaigns during low-sales periods.
* Optimize discount strategies to maximize profitability.
